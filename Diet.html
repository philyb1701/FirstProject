Diet

On this page, I plan to talk about my diet, my techniques and how to deal with hunger.


Food

I mostly stick to the a strict diet of 1200-1400 calories a day, this involves one meal a day usually involving chicken with 100g of rice or pasta, sometimes with a boiled egg or two thrown in. I also have about 2-3 smoothies a day, a Kale/Cucumber Shake, followed by a Protein Shake after my chicken and rice, ending the day with a plate of vegetables.

While on a diet, whether I am cutting weight or bulking for muscle, I get into the mentality that food is a fuel for your body and not something for pleasure.

People sometimes struggle to get vegetables in them, or stuff like Kale. I did too, but once I got a blender - it made things so much easier to get down me. I recommend it to anyone, like me, who finds it hard to stomach vegetables.

Intermittent Fasting

I have also been practicing intermittent fasting, which is basically when you only eat during certain times in a day. Example, a popular method is 16 hours with no food and 8 hours eating. I started out this way and moved to 20:4. This sounds hard, but it is easier than you think.

Why? It is simple. You eat during those hours and those only hours only. I feel better than ever following this technique and have lost a ton of weight in only two months, while maintaining muscle! Take a look below at some of the benefits!

As you can see, it has proven extremely effective. People like X-Men Star Hugh Jackman and MMA legend Georges St-Pierre both have spoken about the positives of IF recently.
